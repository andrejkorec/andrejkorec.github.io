The stretcher bars are made in the joiner workshop from dry spruce wood. The frame is joined without glue, so the canvas could be stretched anytime when necessary.

Precisely treatment of wooden slats ensures high quality of joins. In offer are standard cross-sections frame 20x45 mm, 25x55 mm and 45x45 mm, or other sizes according to needs.

If you have your own idea about it, for example ​​what shape should your stretcher frames have or what cross sections your stretcher bar needs than please feel free to contact me.

According to your order it is possible to construct stretcher bars in any shape or size.
