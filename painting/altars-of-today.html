Reflection on Christianity in Slovak visual arts in the zero years (from 2000 up to now) represents an attempt to search for a meeting point of the two narratives: religious Christian myth and visual arts, focusing on the horizon of the present day and recent past.

My altar of today is made ​​up of memories of moments recorded in notepad. It is a shelf where I imposed my rarest, strongest, nicest moments.

My altar is now my child's playground, a place where we play together, stay together. It is where I hope, ask and believe that I will be a good parent, that I lead my child to be independent, self-esteem and good. There is where I am thankful for every moment spent together.
