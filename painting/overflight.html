Painting instalation Overflight (approx. 550x550cm) is compounded of over 60 different sized formats.

It is based on my personal experience of a bird flock. It represents something enormous, untouchable, something that is beyond man. It is reduced to almost black and white and it is characteristic by minimal brush strokes that created a huge structure.

For me, it was an expresion of power of little things.
